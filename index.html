Software Developer + Team Leader + Project Manager

I am a Costa Rican living in Lafayette, IN, working at Purdue University as Director of Application Development.

At Purdue, I have been given the great opportunity to lead an incredibly smart group of people to define the future of software development for multiple business areas. I enjoy life in general, but mostly when I am reading a good book, spending time with my family or when working on something that I feel is making a difference.
